Chicken

We recommend that you leave that you leave the skin on. After all, without some kind of skin in the mix, what is the point of eating chicken? But wait, we said chicken should be healthy right? Well we will coat that skin in any kind of powder you'd like. That way it will be healthy. At that point we cannot guarantee the taste. It could taste like yesterday's chicken, or maybe even chicken your son didn't agree with! That is a gamble you'll just have to take. Chef's specialty is the chicken gyro. It comes with all the normal fixins. We make our pita in house. You'll have to try it to believe it. We won't make it any way but our way when it comes to the gyro, so ignore everything you just read!
